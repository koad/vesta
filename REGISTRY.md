## Notes

- Gaps in VESTA-SPEC numbering (021–026, 039–043) indicate specs written before this registry existed or assigned via external issue references. They will be catalogued on next audit.
- `constraint-documented` status means the spec documents a deliberate decision boundary, not a gap. See VESTA-SPEC-047 for an example.
- `draft-50k` means the spec is at a high-altitude design stage (50,000-foot view) and is not ready for implementation.
